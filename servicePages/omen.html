Common Issues

Heat Issue

Have you recently added a new ssd?

- Double check to make sure you removed the sticker.


Have you cleaned the inside of your computer?

- Open up the computer and clear out any dust.

Power Issue

Is there any gunk in the power connector slot?

- Make sure to remove any debris to ensure good connection.


Is the power cord connected to the brick?

- The cable tends to slip off when tugged on.

Speed Issue

Is your laptop full on storage?


- Free up some storage, low storage causes certain applications to run slower.

Still not fixed? Send us an email with a short description of the issue and we will return your message within 24 to 48 hours.
